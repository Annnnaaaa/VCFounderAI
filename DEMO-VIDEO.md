# Demo Video Plan (~60s)

**Story arc:** thesis → ranked pipeline → honest diligence → decision. Pure product flow, zero architecture talk (that's the tech video's job).

## Prep

- Clean the pipeline first: delete test rows, dedupe VectorForge — the table is on camera the whole time.
- Pre-open 5 tabs so scene changes are instant Ctrl+Tab switches:
  1. Home
  2. `/thesis`
  3. `/pipeline`
  4. VectorForge detail (`/opportunity/0a7f6ac8-…`)
  5. AgentStack detail (`/opportunity/2db00f67-…`) scrolled to its red claim chips, with its memo one click away
- F11 full screen, 100% zoom, bookmarks bar hidden, notifications off.
- Move the mouse slowly — the cursor is your camera.

## Script (~135 words, rehearse to 55s)

| Time | Scene / action | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| 0–7s | Home, slow scroll past hero | "This is VCFounderAI — an investor's thesis, running as a product." |
| 7–15s | Thesis config: hover sectors, risk slider | "You configure the fund lens once — sectors, stage, check size, risk. Every screen filters through it." |
| 15–27s | Pipeline: scroll table, pause on a GitHub-badge row | "The pipeline ranks everything against that thesis — including founders we discovered on GitHub and Hacker News who never applied. Three separate axes. Never one blended score." |
| 27–40s | VectorForge: axis cards → sparkline → cold-start interval bar | "Open a company: founder, market, and idea each argue their own case. Thin track record? You get an honest interval — not fake confidence." |
| 40–52s | AgentStack: hover a red contradicted chip → memo, past gap flags to recommendation | "And claims get checked: this deck says thirty-K MRR — contradicted, no product to buy. The memo cites every statement, flags every gap, and ends in a decision." |
| 52–60s | Linger on recommendation banner, cut to pipeline | "Diligence at the speed of thought — VCFounderAI." |

## Must land on screen (judge bait)

- Source badges on outbound rows
- The 65→70→75 sparkline
- The cold-start interval bar
- A trust-chip evidence popover flashing open on hover
- An amber gap flag

## Recording

Same as the tech video — OBS or Loom, 1080p, 2–3 takes. If narrating while mousing is awkward, record the click-through silently against the timing column, then voice over it in Clipchamp. Scrolls under 2 seconds; pause a beat wherever the voiceover points at something.
